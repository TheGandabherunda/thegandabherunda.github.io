About Us

The Gandabherunda is a collective dedicated to the art of software creation. We believe in the power of open-source and the fundamental right to digital freedom. Our mission is to build elegant, intuitive, and powerful applications and websites that respect user privacy and are accessible to everyone, completely free of charge.

We operate on a simple principle: software should empower, not exploit. In an era where data has become a commodity, we stand firm in our commitment to never collect or sell user data. Our projects are a testament to this philosophy, crafted with care and a distinct aesthetic style that prioritizes clarity and user experience.

Everything we create is open-source, allowing for transparency, collaboration, and community improvement. We invite you to explore our work, use our software, and join us in championing a digital world that is free, private, and beautifully designed.
